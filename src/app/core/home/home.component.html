I've always loved art and design, even as an engineer. During college, I explored body piercings and found my calling - expressing myself through jewelry. I wanted to unleash my creative spirit and empower others to shine. So I merged my skills into a mission: designing pieces packed with personality. Whether dangling earrings or statement necklaces, each piece tells a story. A lightbulb ignited - I'd craft personalized jewels capturing peoples' very essence. Now my studio is a workshop where confidence is born. Through metal and gems, I help clients manifest themselves. Their grins when gazing at customs works in the mirror fuel me endlessly. My journey brought me from architecture drafts to fueling peoples' joys through adornment. I hope you'll stay sparkling with whatever dreams you choose to bead together.
