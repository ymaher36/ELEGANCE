Who am I?

I am an architect & a gifted artist who discovered my interest in piercings and jewelry during my studies. Then, I embarked on a journey to bring my idea of designing jewelry into life. Inspired by the potential for self-expression, I decided to combine my artistic skills with piercing concepts, aiming to create spaces that brought happiness and empowerment. A profound epiphany led me to the idea of crafting personalized necklaces for my clients, using my artistic abilities to capture their unique personalities.
